Russian Perspective on Security and Multipolarity

Russia asserts itself as indispensable to international security and rejects any security arrangements excluding its participation, considering such talks futile or dead-end. Alongside this, Russian allies are deepening defense ties and supporting multipolar global governance as a counterbalance to Western dominance. Russia emphasizes its critical role in shaping global security architecture even amid tensions with Western countries, demonstrated by statements from high-ranking officials and diplomatic activities such as participation in multilateral forums and regional economic forums involving Russia and affiliated states.
