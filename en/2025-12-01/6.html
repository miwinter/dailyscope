Trump-Maduro Phone Talks Amid Rising US-Venezuela Tensions

Trump-Maduro Talks 2025-12-01 • 36 articles in total

In brief
  • Donald Trump confirmed a rare phone call with Nicolás Maduro amid tense US-Venezuela relations.
  • The call included discussions on exile offers, anti-narcotics efforts, and reflected complex geopolitical strategies.
  • Venezuela condemned US military threats while Maduro sought OPEC support, showing ongoing diplomatic strain.

US President Donald Trump confirmed multiple times that he had a phone conversation with Venezuelan President Nicolás Maduro, marking a rare direct communication between the two leaders amid longstanding tensions. Details of the discussions were largely undisclosed, with Trump describing the talks as neither clearly positive nor negative. Reports suggest the call included offers such as safe passage for Maduro to exile and discussions about potential anti-narcotics operations, reflecting complex geopolitical interests. Venezuela condemned US military buildups and threats, while Maduro sought support from OPEC against perceived aggression. These interactions indicate ongoing diplomatic maneuvering amid escalating political and military strains between the US and Venezuela.
