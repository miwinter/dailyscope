France, alongside Italy, has taken a firm stance in delaying and blocking the EU-Mercosur trade deal due to concerns over economic and environmental impacts, particularly regarding free meat imports from Latin America. This position underscores intra-EU tensions and highlights France's protective approach to its domestic markets and environmental standards, affecting broader EU trade policy and international commerce.
