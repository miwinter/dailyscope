Milei chief of staff resigns amid corruption probe

Argentina Milei Aide Resignation 2026-06-28 • 7 articles in total

In brief
  • Manuel Adorni resigned amid formal corruption investigations and allegations of illicit enrichment.
  • The resignation may destabilize President Milei's government and disrupt his policy agenda.
  • Adorni publicly defended his innocence and criticized media attacks during the scandal.

Manuel Adorni, chief of staff to Argentine President Javier Milei, resigned amid ongoing corruption allegations and investigations into alleged illicit enrichment. Coverage highlights both the formal probes and growing political pressure surrounding the case. Several reports emphasize Adorni’s public defenses — including social media statements asserting his innocence and complaints about relentless media attacks. The resignation has prompted discussion of replacements and raised concerns that the scandal could further destabilize Milei’s agenda and government.
