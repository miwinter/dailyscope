Shooting at Brown University: Multiple Casualties and Ongoing Manhunt

Brown University Shooting 2025-12-14 • 32 articles in total

In brief
  • A shooting at Brown University caused at least two deaths and multiple injuries, leading to lockdowns and panic.
  • The suspect, a man dressed in black, remains at large amid a large-scale manhunt and heightened security.
  • The incident prompted swift emergency response, ongoing investigations, and comments from US President Trump urging intensified efforts.

A shooting at Brown University in Providence, Rhode Island, resulted in at least two deaths and eight to nine serious injuries, causing widespread panic and lockdowns on campus. The suspect, described as a man dressed in black, remains at large, prompting a large-scale manhunt and heightened security measures. Authorities and emergency services responded swiftly, with investigations ongoing to determine the motive and circumstances. The incident has shocked the university community and raised concerns about campus safety and gun violence in educational institutions. US President Donald Trump has publicly commented on the situation, urging intensified efforts to apprehend the suspect.
