Israeli Airstrikes in Gaza Challenge Fragile Ceasefire Amid Rising Casualties

2025-11-23 • 36 articles in total

Recent Israeli airstrikes in Gaza have resulted in significant casualties, with death tolls ranging from 9 to over 30 according to various sources. These attacks have repeatedly violated the fragile ceasefire agreement between Israel and Hamas, escalating tensions and undermining diplomatic efforts for peace. Israeli authorities emphasize targeting Hamas militants, including senior leaders, while Palestinian sources highlight civilian casualties and humanitarian concerns. The international community expresses growing alarm over the violence and its potential to destabilize the region further. Both sides continue to accuse each other of ceasefire breaches, with no clear resolution in sight.
