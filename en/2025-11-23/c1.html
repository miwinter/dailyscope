Macron's Diplomatic Challenges and Domestic Unrest in France

French President Emmanuel Macron faces significant challenges both domestically and internationally. Domestically, his approval ratings have dropped amid public protests against violence and political dissatisfaction, including a rare rejection of the state budget. Internationally, Macron is actively engaged in complex diplomatic efforts, including peace negotiations on Ukraine, strengthening ties with African nations, and addressing territorial disputes, while also managing security concerns in regions like Mali and Marseille.
