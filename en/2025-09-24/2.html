These gradual tactical improvements signify Ukraine's adaptive approach to warfare, compensating for asymmetric power imbalances. Furthermore, Ukrainian strikes on Russian oil refineries, significantly reducing fuel exports, illustrate strategic economic warfare aimed at weakening Russia's financial and military capabilities. This viewpoint highlights Ukraine’s ingenuity and continued resilience in active combat situations.
