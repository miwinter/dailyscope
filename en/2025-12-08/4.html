Escalation of Thailand-Cambodia Border Conflict Amid Fragile Peace

Thailand–Cambodia Border Airstrikes 2025-12-08 • 28 articles in total

In brief
  • Thailand launched airstrikes against Cambodia along their disputed border after border clashes caused casualties on both sides.
  • The airstrikes threaten the ceasefire and peace agreement brokered by U.S. President Trump and raise concerns about regional stability.
  • Both countries blame each other for provocations while diplomatic efforts struggle to de-escalate the conflict.

Thailand has launched multiple airstrikes against Cambodia along their disputed border, escalating long-standing tensions between the two countries. These military actions follow border clashes that resulted in casualties, including the death of soldiers on both sides. The airstrikes threaten the fragile ceasefire and peace agreement brokered by U.S. President Donald Trump, raising concerns about regional stability. Both nations accuse each other of provocations, and diplomatic efforts to de-escalate the conflict face significant challenges. The situation remains volatile with potential for further escalation and international attention focused on preventing wider conflict.
