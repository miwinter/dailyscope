SpaceX IPO and Musk's Trillionaire Milestone

Elon Musk Wealth Milestone 2026-06-12 • 9 articles in total

In brief
  • SpaceX's IPO could make Elon Musk the first trillionaire depending on pricing and market response.
  • The $75 billion IPO is one of the largest ever and reshapes aerospace and tech investment expectations.
  • Regional economies like Tonga should watch the IPO for satellite and digital infrastructure opportunities.

Global coverage centers on SpaceX's record IPO and its outsized implications for Elon Musk's personal wealth and the space-tech sector. Several outlets link the Nasdaq listing and large valuation to claims that Musk could become — or has become, depending on the report — the first trillionaire, while others stress that outcomes depend on final pricing and market reception. Reporting also highlights the IPO's unprecedented scale, with a roughly $75 billion valuation and heavy investor interest framing it as one of the largest market debuts ever. Commentators note the broader effects on the aerospace and tech industries, and some regional pieces urge local economies to watch for opportunities tied to satellite connectivity and the emerging compute economy.
