Dukono eruption: fatalities, missing hikers and ongoing rescue efforts

Indonesia Volcano Rescue 2026-05-09 • 10 articles in total

In brief
  • Mount Dukono eruption caused ash clouds and triggered search-and-rescue operations with casualties and missing hikers reported.
  • Reports vary on the number of missing climbers, including foreign nationals, prompting international concern and coordination.
  • Rescue efforts continue amid dangerous ash and terrain, with confirmed deaths and ongoing evacuations.

Mount Dukono on Halmahera erupted, sending ash high into the sky and triggering urgent search-and-rescue operations. Multiple outlets report three hikers killed while others were rescued or evacuated, but casualty and missing counts vary between reports. Several stories emphasize that dozens of climbers were initially unaccounted for and that some missing are foreign nationals, prompting international concern. Rescue teams continue operations amid hazardous ash, rugged terrain and official warnings to avoid affected areas.
