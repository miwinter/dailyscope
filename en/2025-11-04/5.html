Afghanistan Earthquake Causes Major Casualties and Damage

2025-11-04 • 27 articles in total

A powerful 6.3 magnitude earthquake struck northern Afghanistan, causing significant destruction, killing at least 20 people and injuring hundreds to over a thousand according to some reports. The disaster severely impacted local communities, damaging infrastructure and historic sites such as the Blue Mosque near Mazar-e-Sharif. Emergency and rescue teams from Afghan authorities and international agencies have been mobilized amid difficult terrain and ongoing challenges, including aftershocks and limited access. Several countries and international organizations have expressed condolences and are coordinating humanitarian aid to assist victims. The earthquake compounds existing humanitarian crises in the region, and casualty figures may rise as rescue operations continue.
