Modena car-ramming under investigation

Italy Vehicular Attack Probe 2026-05-17 • 26 articles in total

In brief
  • A driver attacked pedestrians in Modena using a vehicle and a knife, injuring multiple people, some critically.
  • The suspect, named Salim El Koudri, has a reported psychiatric history and is under police investigation for motive.
  • Authorities are probing whether the incident was a targeted attack or an isolated act involving mental health issues.

A driver ploughed into pedestrians in Modena, northern Italy, leaving multiple people injured—most reports cite eight injured, with several in critical condition and at least one facing possible amputation. Witnesses and some outlets describe the suspect exiting the vehicle armed with a knife and assaulting bystanders, while authorities say the driver was detained and investigators are probing motive. Some coverage profiles the suspect, named in Italian reports as Salim El Koudri, noting a psychiatric history and personal background as police continue questioning. International media emphasize public safety concerns and the ongoing inquiry into whether the incident was a targeted attack or an isolated act by an individual with mental-health issues.
