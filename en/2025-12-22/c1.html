Multiple articles report on France's announcement and initiation of constructing a new, larger, and nuclear-powered aircraft carrier to replace the aging Charles de Gaulle. This project is a centerpiece of France's defense modernization strategy, aiming to enhance naval capabilities, power projection, and maintain strategic autonomy amid evolving global security challenges. The carrier will be equipped with advanced technology, including drone capabilities, and is expected to enter service by 2038, underscoring France's long-term military investment.
