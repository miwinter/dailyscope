Foiled domestic terror plots targeting Trump and White House events

US Domestic Terror Plot 2026-06-17 • 10 articles in total

In brief
  • Federal agencies disrupted multiple domestic terror plots targeting Donald Trump and a UFC/MMA event near the White House.
  • Suspects planned attacks using drones, explosives, and firearms around Trump's 80th birthday and the UFC/MMA show.
  • Investigations continue into wider extremist networks exploiting remote weapons against political figures and public events.

U.S. federal authorities including the FBI and Department of Justice say they have disrupted multiple domestic terror plots aimed at President Donald Trump and a high-profile MMA/UFC event near the White House. Reports describe arrests and indictments of several suspects alleged to have planned attacks around Trump’s 80th birthday and a UFC/MMA show, using drones, explosives and firearms. Officials have portrayed the cases as serious domestic security threats with investigations continuing to determine wider networks and motives. The incidents highlight growing concerns about extremists leveraging remote weapons like drones alongside conventional arms to target political figures and mass gatherings.
