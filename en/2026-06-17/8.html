Eduardo Bolsonaro convicted for coercion

Brazil Judicial Coercion Verdict 2026-06-17 • 10 articles in total

Brazil's Supreme Court has convicted Eduardo Bolsonaro, son of former president Jair Bolsonaro, of coercion and obstruction and sentenced him to just over four years in prison with potential additional bans on holding office. International coverage presents the verdict as both enforcement of judicial independence and a significant moment in broader political and legal scrutiny of the Bolsonaro family. Regional and domestic reporting highlights immediate political fallout, public reactions, possible appeals, and the implications for ongoing investigations into the former president and his allies.
