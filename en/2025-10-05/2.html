Trump’s Gaza Diplomacy: Calls for Ceasefire Amidst Continued Conflict and Diplomatic Deadlocks

2025-10-05 • 70 articles in total

Former U.S. President Donald Trump has been actively engaged in mediating peace efforts in the Gaza conflict by proposing a ceasefire plan that hinges on Hamas’s approval and an Israeli troop withdrawal. Trump has repeatedly called on Israel to halt bombings immediately following Hamas's indications of willingness to negotiate, including the release of hostages, although Israeli military operations have largely continued despite these calls. Hamas's responses have ranged from cautious acceptance to demands for amendments, while the U.S. has maintained a firm stance against delays in implementation. International reactions vary, reflecting a tense and volatile environment where diplomatic efforts coexist with ongoing violence and humanitarian concerns.
