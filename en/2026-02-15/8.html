US Military Prepares for Extended Operations Against Iran Amid Rising Tensions

US Preps Iran Strike 2026-02-15 • 13 articles in total

In brief
  • The US is preparing for potentially weeks-long military operations targeting Iran's nuclear sites and regime core.
  • Diplomatic talks in Geneva continue alongside military planning, showing a dual approach of pressure and negotiation.
  • US actions occur within a complex global context involving pressures on Iran, regional security concerns, and broader geopolitical tensions.

The United States is preparing for potential military operations against Iran that could last several weeks, reflecting escalating tensions and heightened military readiness. These preparations include plans targeting not only Iran's nuclear facilities but also the core of its regime, signaling a significant escalation beyond previous focus areas. While military deployments and strategic planning advance, diplomatic efforts continue with upcoming talks scheduled in Geneva, highlighting a dual approach of pressure and negotiation. US leadership, including President Trump, has emphasized regime change and warned Iran of strong responses to provocations. The situation underscores fragile regional security and the complexity of balancing military action with ongoing diplomacy.
