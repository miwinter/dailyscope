France, under President Emmanuel Macron's leadership, is actively responding to escalating tensions in the Middle East, particularly around the Strait of Hormuz, by preparing and deploying naval forces to secure vital maritime routes and protect French nationals. Macron is also spearheading diplomatic efforts, including calls for ceasefires and organizing international meetings on energy security and nuclear power. Domestically, France faces significant political developments such as the sentencing of former President Nicolas Sarkozy, while continuing to maintain diplomatic relations globally, including appointments and overseas visits.
