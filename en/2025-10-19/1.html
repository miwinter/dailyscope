Diverging Perspectives on US-Russia-Ukraine Conflict and Peace Efforts

2025-10-19 • 115 articles in total

The collection of articles reveals sharply contrasting viewpoints regarding the ongoing conflict in Ukraine and the role of the US and Russia. Many articles highlight former US President Donald Trump's reluctance to supply Ukraine with Tomahawk missiles, favoring diplomatic negotiations and conflict freeze over military escalation, which has caused disappointment and concern among Ukrainian officials and supporters. Russian perspectives emphasize territorial demands, especially control over Donetsk, and frame Ukrainian military actions as provocations, while European and other international sources push for sustained support to Ukraine and just peace solutions. The anticipated Trump-Putin summit in Budapest is seen by some as a potential breakthrough for peace, albeit with skepticism from other analysts who doubt major progress. Meanwhile, ongoing violence, infrastructural damage, and human rights abuses continue to challenge diplomatic efforts and shape the conflict's trajectory.
