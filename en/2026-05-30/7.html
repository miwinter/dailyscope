Galați drone incident: diplomatic moves and NATO calls

Romania Drone Incident 2026-05-30 • 9 articles in total

In brief
  • A drone struck a residential building in Galați, Romania, injuring two people and prompting an official investigation.
  • Romania expelled a Russian diplomat, closed a Russian consulate, and NATO members condemned the incident calling for faster air-defense support.
  • Brussels moved toward coordinated sanctions, highlighting wider EU and NATO political and security consequences.

A drone struck a residential building in Galați, Romania, injuring two people and prompting an official investigation. Romania responded with diplomatic measures, expelling a Russian diplomat and closing a Russian consulate, while local authorities reported on casualties and damage. NATO members and regional leaders condemned the incident, urging faster air-defence support and discussing possible invocation of NATO Article 4 to consult allies. Brussels also moved toward coordinated sanctions, underscoring wider EU and NATO political and security repercussions.
