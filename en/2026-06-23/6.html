Deadly Montreal shooting leaves officer and suspect dead; motive under investigation

Montreal Police Shooting 2026-06-23 • 19 articles in total

In brief
  • A shooting in Montreal killed three people including a police officer and the attacker, who was shot dead by officers on the scene.
  • Authorities are investigating a possible motive linked to a manifesto and the incel movement but have not confirmed it.
  • The incident occurred in a residential area, including a Jewish neighbourhood, causing community concern and calls for calm.

A shooting in Montreal left three people dead, including a police officer and the suspected gunman, and several others were reported injured. Responding officers shot and killed the attacker at the scene and authorities say there is no ongoing threat to the public. Investigators have launched a broad inquiry into the motive, with some reports noting a surrendered manifesto and possible links to the “incel” movement while others say no motive has been confirmed. The event occurred in a residential area including reports from a Jewish neighbourhood and has prompted heightened community concern and calls for calm as evidence is collected.
