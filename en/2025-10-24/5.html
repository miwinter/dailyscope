Trump and Xi to Meet in South Korea Amid High-Stakes Diplomacy

2025-10-24 • 30 articles in total

Former US President Donald Trump and Chinese President Xi Jinping are scheduled to meet on October 30, 2025, in South Korea during an Asia tour coinciding with an international summit. The meeting is highly anticipated as it aims to address key bilateral issues including trade tensions, security concerns such as North Korea, and broader geopolitical challenges. Both leaders express cautious optimism about engaging in dialogue despite ongoing economic and political disputes. The summit is viewed as a critical event for regional stability and global diplomacy, with implications for US-China relations and international markets. Details about the full agenda remain limited, but the encounter signals renewed efforts to manage complex relations between the two superpowers.
