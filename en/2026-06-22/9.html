Deadly attack at Niamey airport prompts regional condemnation

Niger Terror Attack 2026-06-22 • 8 articles in total

In brief
  • A coordinated attack with explosions and gunfire hit Niamey International Airport causing multiple deaths including soldiers and civilians.
  • Regional and international groups condemned the assault and called for unified action against jihadist threats in Niger and the Sahel.
  • Authorities are investigating the attack amid ongoing security challenges and uncertainty about casualty figures.

A coordinated assault at Niamey International Airport involved explosions and sustained gunfire, with multiple reports describing a prolonged attack on the facility. Several outlets reported a death toll around 35 while at least one report specified 11 soldiers and 2 civilians among the dead; other accounts noted ongoing operations and unclear casualty figures. Regional groups, the African Union and civil society quickly condemned the violence, expressed solidarity with Niger and urged coordinated action against militants. Authorities are investigating and securing the scene, highlighting persistent jihadist threats and broader insecurity across Niger and the Sahel.
