Deadly Antwerp apartment fire prompts investigation

Belgium Fire Investigation 2026-07-02 • 7 articles in total

In brief
  • A severe fire in a 10-storey apartment building in Antwerp killed at least five people and injured others.
  • Firefighters evacuated residents, conducted rescue operations, and searches for victims continue.
  • Authorities are investigating the fire's cause and assessing building safety and regulatory compliance.

A severe fire tore through a multi-storey apartment building in Antwerp, killing at least five people and injuring others, with some reports indicating up to six fatalities. Firefighters and emergency services mounted large rescue and search operations, evacuating residents and treating the wounded. Authorities have launched investigations into the blaze’s cause and are assessing the building’s safety and regulatory compliance amid concerns about housing standards. Coverage varies on the confirmed death toll and ongoing searches as officials continue to work at the scene.
