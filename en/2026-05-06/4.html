Fighting, fragile truces and widening fallout around Victory Day

Russo-Ukrainian War 2026-05-06 • 70 articles in total

In brief
  • Intense fighting in Ukraine causes many civilian casualties despite announced short truces linked to Victory Day.
  • Western countries increase sanctions on Russia and supply Ukraine with weapons amid diplomatic efforts and humanitarian concerns.
  • Ukrainian forces conduct long-range strikes deep into Russian territory, heightening regional security worries.

Reports show intense fighting across Ukraine with numerous civilian casualties even as both Moscow and Kyiv announced short truces tied to Victory Day commemorations. Long-range strikes and drone attacks have reached far into Russian territory, prompting heightened security in Russian cities and sharp rhetoric from the Kremlin. Western partners continue to tighten sanctions and supply Ukraine with weapons while debating risks and worst‑case scenarios. Diplomacy is active but cautious: phone calls and international appeals for unity accompany warnings about sanctions evasion, growing humanitarian needs, and the fragility of any ceasefire.
