Typhoon Fung-wong Devastates Philippines with Fatalities and Massive Evacuations

2025-11-10 • 26 articles in total

Super Typhoon Fung-wong made landfall in the Philippines, causing at least two deaths and forcing the evacuation of approximately one to 1.4 million people. The storm triggered widespread damage, flooding, landslides, and power outages particularly in northern and eastern regions. Authorities and emergency services across all reports have been engaged in rescue, evacuation, and relief operations while urging residents to follow safety protocols. The typhoon struck while the country was still recovering from recent deadly storms, amplifying the challenges faced by Philippine communities. Recovery efforts are ongoing amid warnings of ongoing severe weather threats.
