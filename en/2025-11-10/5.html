Historic US-Syria Diplomatic Thaw Marked by Al-Sharaa's Visit

2025-11-10 • 28 articles in total

Syrian President Ahmed al-Sharaa's official visit to Washington, following his removal from the US terrorism blacklist, represents a historic and unprecedented diplomatic engagement between Syria and the United States. This visit marks the first by a Syrian president to the White House since 1946 and signals a potential shift from hostility to cooperation. Key discussions during the visit include counterterrorism efforts, regional security, bilateral relations, and pathways towards peace in the Middle East. The meeting with former President Donald Trump has attracted significant international attention, reflecting evolving geopolitical dynamics and hopes for a new chapter in US-Syria relations. While the visit is celebrated as a breakthrough, al-Sharaa's controversial past adds complexity to the diplomatic warming.
