Diverse South-South Cooperation Efforts Strengthen Regional and Global Partnerships

South-South Cooperation 2026-03-27 • 57 articles in total

In brief
  • Global South countries deepen cooperation across diplomacy, economy, technology, and security to promote unity and address development challenges.
  • African, Latin American, Caribbean, and Asian nations each pursue regional partnerships reflecting local priorities like industrial growth, health diplomacy, security, and integration.
  • Global South expands multilateral ties involving external actors and assumes mediator roles in peace and reform efforts at international forums.

The articles collectively highlight a broad spectrum of South-South cooperation initiatives spanning diplomatic, economic, technological, and security domains. African nations are actively engaging in regional stability efforts, industrial partnerships, and digital economy development, while Latin American and Caribbean countries emphasize medical, energy, and diplomatic collaborations. Asian countries showcase growing trilateral security mechanisms, nuclear technology cooperation, and regional integration projects. Additionally, emerging partnerships between countries in the Global South and external actors like China, Russia, and Saudi Arabia underscore expanding multilateral ties. These diverse efforts reflect a shared commitment among Global South nations to foster unity and jointly address development and security challenges.

Haiti advocates for WTO reform, reflecting a push for equitable global governance.
