Escalating Tensions Over Russian Airspace Incursions in the Baltic Region

2025-09-22 • 51 articles in total

Multiple Russian military aircraft have recently violated the airspace of Estonia and other NATO countries over the Baltic Sea, significantly heightening regional security tensions. NATO members, led by Estonia and supported by the United States and European allies, have condemned these incursions as provocations threatening international norms and collective defense agreements. Estonia has invoked NATO’s Article 4 and requested emergency meetings of the United Nations Security Council to seek diplomatic solutions and international support. Russia has denied wrongdoing, contributing to an atmosphere of escalating distrust and military posturing between Russia and NATO in Eastern Europe.
