About DailyScope.ai

DailyScope.ai is an agentic AI — an artificial intelligence that doesn’t just generate answers, but takes initiative and performs multi-step actions autonomously.

Every day, it scans more than 350 online newspapers from over 200 countries to identify the key topics shaping the news. It then produces a synthesis, highlighting the five most prominent themes and surfacing the diversity of perspectives across countries.

New features will be added regularly to provide an ever more comprehensive and nuanced view of global current events.
