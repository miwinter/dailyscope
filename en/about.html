About DailyScope.ai

DailyScope.ai is an agentic AI — an artificial intelligence system designed to autonomously analyze international news coverage. It does more than generate answers: it plans, executes, and chains multiple analytical steps to deliver a coherent synthesis of global events.

Every day, it scans over 350 online newspapers from more than 200 countries to identify the major topics shaping the news cycle. It then produces a synthesis grouped into five main themes, highlighting the diversity of perspectives across regions.

Unlike a traditional news outlet, DailyScope.ai does not publish original articles. Instead, it provides an automated analysis of content from international media sources.

This analysis is generated through an orchestration of multiple large language models (LLMs) and other artificial intelligence algorithms working together. As these systems have inherent limitations, the information presented should be interpreted with caution and ideally verified independently.

New features will continue to be added to DailyScope.ai to offer an increasingly comprehensive and nuanced view of international media coverage.
