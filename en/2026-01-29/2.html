Tragic Plane Crash in Colombia Claims 15 Lives Including Politicians

Colombia Plane Crash Investigation 2026-01-29 • 41 articles in total

In brief
  • A plane crash in northeastern Colombia killed all 15 people onboard, including a congressman and a candidate.
  • The crash occurred in mountainous terrain, complicating rescue efforts and sparking investigations into aviation safety.
  • The tragedy deeply affected Colombia's political community and drew international attention and condolences.

A plane crash in northeastern Colombia resulted in the deaths of all 15 people on board, including a sitting congressman and a congressional candidate. The aircraft, operated by the state-owned airline Satena, disappeared from radar shortly before reaching its destination, prompting extensive search and rescue operations in a mountainous and challenging terrain. The tragedy has deeply impacted the Colombian political community and raised serious concerns about aviation safety in the region. Authorities are conducting thorough investigations to determine the cause of the crash and to prevent future incidents. Rescue teams confirmed no survivors, and the incident has drawn international attention and condolences.
