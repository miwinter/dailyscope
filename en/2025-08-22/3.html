2025-08-22 • 50 articles in total

Recent natural disasters across the globe include significant earthquakes near Antarctica and South America, devastating floods in Pakistan and parts of Asia, severe hurricanes affecting the U.S. East Coast, and record-breaking wildfires in Europe. Countries close to seismic zones emphasize monitoring, tsunami warnings, and emergency preparedness, while nations impacted by flooding stress humanitarian crises and calls for international aid. European and North African countries highlight the escalating threat of climate-induced wildfires and advocate for regional cooperation. The varying disaster types underline the multifaceted challenges posed by climate change and geological activity worldwide.
