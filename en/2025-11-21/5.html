Escalation of Israeli Airstrikes in Gaza and Lebanon Amid Fragile Ceasefire

2025-11-21 • 33 articles in total

Recent Israeli airstrikes have intensified in both Gaza and southern Lebanon, resulting in significant civilian casualties and infrastructure damage. Despite a fragile ceasefire brokered in October, multiple attacks have killed dozens, including children, and have been widely condemned by international actors and mediators such as Qatar and the United Nations. Hamas and Palestinian authorities accuse Israel of violating ceasefire agreements and escalating violence, while Israel cites retaliatory actions against militant groups. The strikes have heightened regional tensions, threatening peace efforts and raising concerns over a broader conflict involving Hezbollah in Lebanon. The humanitarian situation remains critical, with calls for restraint and renewed diplomatic engagement growing urgent.
