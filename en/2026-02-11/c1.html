France is navigating complex geopolitical tensions, particularly within the European Union and in relations with Russia and the United States. President Emmanuel Macron is advocating for European strategic autonomy, pushing for economic reforms, common debt instruments, and renewed dialogue with Russia, while facing resistance from Germany and rising intra-EU divisions. Domestically, France is confronting serious criminal investigations involving transnational abuse cases and drug trafficking, alongside significant military preparedness exercises. Additionally, France is engaging in international cooperation projects, such as supporting Libya's economic reforms and enhancing connectivity with Kyrgyzstan.
