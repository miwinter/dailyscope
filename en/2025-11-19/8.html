Trump's Defense of Saudi Crown Prince Amid Khashoggi Murder Controversy

2025-11-19 • 15 articles in total

Former U.S. President Donald Trump has consistently defended Saudi Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman against accusations related to the 2018 murder of journalist Jamal Khashoggi. Trump downplayed the Crown Prince's involvement, asserting he was unaware of the killing and dismissing the incident as an unfortunate event. This stance contradicts U.S. intelligence assessments and has sparked international debate about accountability and human rights. Trump's defense is closely tied to maintaining strategic and economic ties between the U.S. and Saudi Arabia, highlighted by significant Saudi investments in the U.S. The controversy continues to influence diplomatic relations and public discourse on foreign policy and press freedom.
