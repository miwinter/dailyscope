Global Impact and Recovery from Cloudflare Outage

2025-11-19 • 11 articles in total

A significant outage at Cloudflare disrupted approximately 20% of the global internet, affecting millions of users and numerous major websites such as X, ChatGPT, Canva, and Facebook. This incident highlighted the vulnerabilities and dependencies on centralized internet infrastructure and cloud service providers. Users worldwide experienced widespread connectivity issues, with many platforms going offline simultaneously. Cloudflare has since restored normal operations and is investigating the root cause, described as a bug or latent error in their systems. The event underscores the critical role of infrastructure providers and the cascading effects of their failures on global internet accessibility.
