France's Strategic Military Support to Ukraine Amid Rising Geopolitical Tensions

France has significantly increased its military support to Ukraine by agreeing to supply up to 100 Rafale fighter jets along with drones and air defense systems, marking a historic boost to Ukraine's defense capabilities amid ongoing conflict with Russia. This move has drawn strong criticism from Russia, which accuses France of escalating the war, while France continues to affirm its commitment to Ukraine's sovereignty. Additionally, France is actively engaged in broader geopolitical and security efforts, including maintaining NATO presence in Eastern Europe, addressing internal security concerns, and pursuing diplomatic initiatives in Africa and the Middle East.
