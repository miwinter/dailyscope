International Perspectives on the Israel-Hamas Hostage Deal and Ceasefire

2025-10-12 • 43 articles in total

A major breakthrough in the Israel-Hamas conflict has emerged with an agreement for a ceasefire and phased release of Israeli hostages by Hamas, endorsed by the Israeli government. The deal, closely tied to U.S. diplomatic efforts, especially under former President Donald Trump, has generated cautious optimism internationally. While Israel insists on excluding prominent Palestinian prisoners like Marwan Barghouti from release, Hamas affirms the start of hostage releases, with ceasefire implementation allowing limited humanitarian access and encouraging return of Palestinian civilians. However, tensions remain high as Hamas fighters continue mobilizing, negotiations are delicate, and the broader conflict dynamics remain fragile with key details on timing and prisoner exchanges still developing.
