  • Pilgrims performed key rites at Arafat and Muzdalifah, with religious leaders promoting unity and prayers for the Muslim community.

The 2026 Hajj has drawn well over a million pilgrims to Mecca, with authorities and international observers praising Saudi organisation and large-scale logistical support. Reports repeatedly highlight extreme heat and public‑health concerns, alongside heightened security measures and regional tensions that frame this year’s pilgrimage. Across accounts, officials stress crowd management and safety protocols to protect worshippers during the peak days of the pilgrimage.
