Severe Tornado Devastates Southern Brazil Causing Multiple Fatalities and Hundreds Injured

2025-11-09 • 21 articles in total

A powerful tornado struck southern Brazil, primarily affecting the state of Paraná and the town of Rio Bonito do Iguaçu, resulting in widespread destruction. The disaster caused the deaths of five to six people and injured between 400 to 750 residents according to various reports. Emergency services swiftly mobilized rescue and relief operations, while local authorities declared states of emergency or calamity in multiple municipalities. The tornado's winds reached extreme speeds of up to 250 km/h, demolishing homes, uprooting trees, and overturning vehicles. This event underscores the vulnerability of the region to extreme weather phenomena and the need for sustained recovery efforts.
