Louisiana mass shooting kills eight children

Louisiana Mass Shooting 2026-04-20 • 23 articles in total

In brief
  • A mass shooting in Shreveport, Louisiana killed eight children and wounded others, with the suspect reportedly a family member who died during the police response.
  • The incident highlights domestic tragedy aspects and has intensified debates on U.S. gun violence and child casualties.
  • Authorities continue investigating and urge the public to provide information while media cover operational details and broader gun control issues.

A mass shooting in Shreveport, Louisiana left eight children dead and multiple others wounded, with victims reported as young as infants and up to early teens. Several outlets report the suspect was a family member — in many accounts the father of seven of the victims — and that the alleged shooter died during the police response or a subsequent chase. Authorities are continuing an active investigation, releasing limited details while collecting evidence and urging the public to come forward with information. The incident has intensified international coverage of U.S. gun violence and prompted renewed debate about child casualties and firearm policy this year.
