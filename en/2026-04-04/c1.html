France's International Legal and Political Stances

France maintains a cautious and independent stance in international legal and political matters, exemplified by denying extradition requests related to Tunisia and advocating for a 'third way' in global power dynamics to reduce reliance on the U.S. and China. Macron proposes coalitions for strategic autonomy and emphasizes diplomacy over military action in conflict zones. These positions reflect France's evolving role in global governance and its desire to assert European leadership and sovereignty.
