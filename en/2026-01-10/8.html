Meloni Advocates EU-Russia Dialogue to Resolve Ukraine Conflict

Meloni Urges EU–Russia Talks 2026-01-10 • 9 articles in total

In brief
  • Italian Prime Minister Giorgia Meloni urges the EU to hold direct talks with Russia to resolve the Ukraine war.
  • Meloni proposes diplomatic mechanisms like appointing a European envoy alongside supporting Ukraine.
  • Italy leads a pragmatic EU approach balancing negotiation efforts with pressure on Russia for peace.

Italian Prime Minister Giorgia Meloni has consistently urged the European Union to initiate direct talks with Russia to help resolve the ongoing war in Ukraine. She emphasizes the necessity of diplomatic engagement alongside support for Ukraine, proposing mechanisms such as appointing a European envoy to facilitate negotiations. Meloni's stance reflects a pragmatic approach within the EU, contrasting with more hardline policies that prioritize sanctions and military support. This call for dialogue highlights a growing debate in Europe about balancing pressure on Russia with efforts to achieve peace through negotiation. The position underscores Italy's influential role in shaping EU policy amid complex geopolitical tensions.
