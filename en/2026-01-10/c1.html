Storm Goretti and Severe Weather Impact

France has been severely affected by Storm Goretti, which caused widespread power outages impacting hundreds of thousands of households, disrupted nuclear power plant operations, and challenged emergency response systems. The storm, along with other severe weather events like Storm Elli, has exposed vulnerabilities in France's infrastructure and highlighted the increasing frequency of extreme weather in Europe. Authorities are actively working to restore services and mitigate further damage.

Trade and Economic Policy: Opposition to EU-Mercosur Agreement

President Macron has publicly opposed the EU-Mercosur trade agreement due to concerns over environmental and economic impacts, reflecting significant domestic opposition including from French farmers. Despite France's stance, the agreement received qualified majority approval within the EU, indicating tensions between national interests and broader European trade policies. This opposition illustrates France's prioritization of sustainability and protection of its agricultural sector.
