  • Flight-tracking data and eyewitness videos circulated widely, prompting scrutiny of urban aviation safety and ongoing investigations.

A small aircraft struck Beijing's tallest skyscraper (Citic Tower), sending debris onto surrounding streets and triggering large-scale emergency responses. Authorities sealed off the area, evacuated occupants and reported injuries while rescue teams assessed damage and casualties. Eyewitness video and flight-tracking data circulated internationally, prompting scrutiny of urban aviation safety and air-traffic procedures. Official statements remain limited as investigators examine flight records, weather and safety protocols and prepare further updates.
