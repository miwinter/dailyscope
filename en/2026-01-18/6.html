Disappearance and Crash of Indonesian Plane with 10-11 Onboard: Search and Recovery Efforts

Indonesia Missing Flight 2026-01-18 • 25 articles in total

In brief
  • An Indonesian plane with 10 to 11 people disappeared over Sulawesi and was found crashed in difficult terrain.
  • Rescue teams face challenges searching for survivors and recovering bodies in mountainous, forested areas.
  • The crash raises serious concerns about aviation safety and ongoing investigations are underway.

An Indonesian aircraft carrying 10 to 11 people disappeared from radar over Sulawesi or eastern Indonesia, prompting extensive search and rescue operations involving helicopters, drones, and ground teams. Wreckage has been found in mountainous or forested areas, with some reports confirming recovery of bodies but no survivors. The incident has raised significant concerns about aviation safety, especially in remote and challenging terrain. Authorities continue investigations to determine the cause of the crash while focusing on recovery efforts amid difficult conditions. The event highlights ongoing risks in Indonesian aviation and surveillance missions.
