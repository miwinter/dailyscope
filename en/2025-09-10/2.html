Escalation and Divergent International Reactions to Israeli Airstrikes on Hamas in Qatar

2025-09-10 • 133 articles in total

Israel’s unprecedented military airstrikes targeting Hamas leadership in Doha, Qatar, mark a significant escalation in the Middle East conflict, expanding beyond traditional zones like Gaza. The attacks have resulted in casualties including Hamas leaders and Qatari security personnel, provoking strong condemnations from Qatar, many Arab states, and the international community, while Israel defends the operations as necessary counter-terrorism measures. The strikes have also triggered diplomatic tensions involving key global actors such as the United States, which exhibited mixed responses ranging from prior notification claims to cautious disapproval, alongside vocal criticisms from former US President Donald Trump and various European countries. The conflict dynamics are further complicated by retaliatory actions from Iran and allied militant groups, increasing regional instability and challenging diplomatic efforts toward ceasefire and peace mediation.
