Political Turmoil in Nepal: Youth-led Protests, Social Media Bans, and Government Resignations

2025-09-10 • 65 articles in total

Nepal has been engulfed in widespread protests predominantly led by Generation Z, triggered by government-imposed social media bans and underlying issues of corruption and nepotism. The unrest grew violent, resulting in over 20 deaths, multiple injuries, and significant destruction including the burning of the parliament and officials’ homes. Amid escalating political instability, Prime Minister KP Sharma Oli resigned, and the Nepalese army assumed control to stabilize the country. International reactions range from concerned advisories urging caution to calls for peaceful resolution, reflecting the global anxiety over Nepal's unfolding crisis.
