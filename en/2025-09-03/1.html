Humanitarian Crisis and International Responses to Devastating Natural Disasters in Afghanistan and Sudan

2025-09-03 • 111 articles in total

A series of catastrophic natural disasters have recently struck Afghanistan and Sudan, with a deadly earthquake in eastern Afghanistan causing over 1,400 deaths and thousands of injuries, and a massive landslide in western Sudan resulting in approximately 1,000 fatalities. Rescue and relief operations are ongoing amid difficult terrain, infrastructure challenges, and ongoing conflicts, which complicate access and aid delivery. The international community, including the United Nations and various countries, has pledged humanitarian assistance, while affected governments and authorities urgently call for more support. These disasters underscore the vulnerability of conflict-affected and disaster-prone regions, highlighting the importance of global solidarity and coordinated relief efforts.
