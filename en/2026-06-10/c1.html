The project, originally launched to enhance Europe's military capabilities, has been abandoned due to disagreements and shifting strategic priorities, with Germany signaling intentions to pursue independent development. This development highlights challenges in multinational defense procurement and raises questions about the future of European strategic autonomy and military innovation.
