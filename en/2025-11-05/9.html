Multiple Drone Sightings Lead to Airport Closures in Belgium Amid Security Concerns

2025-11-05 • 19 articles in total

A series of unauthorized drone sightings over Brussels and Liège airports have resulted in multiple temporary closures, causing significant flight cancellations and disruptions in Belgian air traffic. Authorities across several countries indicate heightened security measures and ongoing investigations to identify the drone operators and prevent future incidents. Perspectives vary on the origins and motivations of the drone incursions, with some attributing them to wider geopolitical tensions, notably linked to the Ukraine-Russia conflict, while others focus on the increasing threat of spy or surveillance drones near critical infrastructure. The incidents have raised widespread alarm about airport vulnerability and the challenges of regulating drone activity near sensitive airspace. Countries emphasize the need for enhanced security protocols and international cooperation to address emerging aerial threats.
