Deadly Typhoon Kalmaegi Devastates the Philippines, Leaving Widespread Destruction and High Death Toll

2025-11-05 • 34 articles in total

Typhoon Kalmaegi (also spelled Kammaegi, Kamalagi) has caused catastrophic damage across the Philippines, with reported fatalities ranging from 26 to over 90. The storm led to severe flooding, landslides, and widespread displacement, particularly affecting provinces still recovering from recent natural disasters like earthquakes. Rescue and relief operations struggle amid ongoing weather challenges, complicated by incidents such as the downing of a helicopter crew involved in emergency responses. The disaster has prompted national and international concern, further heightened by the typhoon's progression toward flood-affected Vietnam, raising alarm for additional humanitarian crisis. Authorities continue to assess the full impact, coordinate aid, and prepare for subsequent storms threatening the region.
