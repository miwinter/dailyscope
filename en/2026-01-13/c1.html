Military Recruitment and Security Strategy

France is actively enhancing its national defense by launching recruitment drives for voluntary military service, reversing the post-1996 abolition of mandatory conscription. This initiative aims to build a robust reserve force in response to escalating security challenges in Europe. Additionally, France, alongside the UK, plans to deploy troops to Ukraine contingent on a peace agreement with Russia, signaling a commitment to regional stability and deterrence against future aggression.
