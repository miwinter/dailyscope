Zelensky-Trump Summit: Diplomatic Efforts Amid Ukraine Conflict

Zelensky–Trump Summit 2025-12-27 • 43 articles in total

In brief
  • Zelensky will meet Trump to discuss peace plans and security amid the Ukraine-Russia conflict.
  • Western allies support Ukraine's diplomatic efforts while Trump asserts control over agreement approvals.
  • Russia opposes the talks, seeing them as Western interference that complicates peace efforts.

Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelensky is set to meet U.S. President Donald Trump in Florida to discuss peace plans and security guarantees amid the ongoing war with Russia. The meeting is viewed as a significant diplomatic effort to advance negotiations, with topics including territorial issues, ceasefire proposals, and international cooperation. While Zelensky emphasizes progress and unity with European allies, Trump asserts his influential role in approving any agreements. Russia criticizes these talks, underscoring the geopolitical tensions surrounding the conflict. Overall, the summit reflects continued high-level engagement aimed at resolving the Ukraine crisis through diplomacy.
