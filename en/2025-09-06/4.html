Venezuelan Government’s Defensive and Escalatory Stance

The Venezuelan government, led by President Nicolás Maduro, adopts a defensive yet firm stance, condemning US military actions as provocations and violations of sovereignty. Maduro publicly rejects the justification for military conflict while simultaneously warning that Venezuela would escalate to armed struggle if attacked. He calls for dialogue to de-escalate tensions but remains resolute against perceived regime change efforts and foreign interference. Venezuela accuses the US of ulterior motives related to resource control and maintains a posture of readiness in face of US military buildup.
