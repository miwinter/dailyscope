Rio helicopter collision kills six, including Oliver Tree

Rio Helicopter Crash 2026-06-15 • 33 articles in total

In brief
  • Two helicopters collided over western Rio de Janeiro, killing all six aboard, including celebrities from the US and Argentina.
  • Social media widely shared videos and photos of the crash and its aftermath, sparking fan and community reactions.
  • Authorities launched formal investigations amid concerns about urban air-traffic safety and potential protocol reviews.

Two helicopters collided mid-air over western Rio de Janeiro, igniting fires and scattering debris into nearby buildings. All six people aboard both aircraft were killed; among the confirmed victims were American singer Oliver Tree and Argentine YouTuber Gaspi, along with other influencers and a filmmaker. Videos and photos circulated widely on social media while emergency crews secured the scene and authorities opened formal investigations. International reporting has alternated between celebrity-focused coverage and technical accounts, raising questions about urban air-traffic safety and potential protocol reviews.
