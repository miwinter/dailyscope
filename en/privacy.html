Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 18, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how DailyScope.ai (the “Service”) collects and processes personal data. DailyScope.ai is a personal project operated by an individual (“Operator”).

1. Data controller

Data controller: The Operator of DailyScope.ai (individual)
Contact: Contact form

2. What the Service does

DailyScope.ai provides automated analysis of international news coverage using software algorithms and AI models. The Service presents aggregated insights (e.g., themes, trends, rankings) derived from publicly available sources.

3. Personal data we collect

3.1 Data you provide

When you contact us via the contact form, we may collect:

  • Your email address
  • Your message content (and any information you choose to include)
  • Technical metadata related to the submission (e.g., timestamp)

3.2 Data collected automatically

When you browse the Service, limited technical/usage data may be collected, such as:

  • Pages viewed and interactions
  • Approximate location (country/city level, derived from IP)
  • Device and browser information
  • Referral source
  • Identifiers stored in cookies or similar technologies (where applicable)

4. Cookies and analytics

We use analytics and tag management tools (for example Google Analytics 4 and Google Tag Manager) to measure usage and improve the Service. These tools may set cookies or use similar technologies depending on your configuration and consent choices.

Where required by applicable law, non-essential cookies/trackers are only used after you provide consent. You can also manage cookies through your browser settings.

5. Purposes of processing

  • To operate and maintain the Service
  • To respond to your messages and support requests
  • To understand usage and improve performance (analytics)
  • To secure the Service and prevent abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations

6. Legal bases (GDPR)

When the GDPR applies, processing is based on:

  • Legitimate interests (running and securing the Service, responding to messages, basic measurement)
  • Consent (for non-essential cookies/trackers where required)
  • Legal obligation (when required by law)

7. Recipients

We may share personal data with trusted service providers acting as processors (e.g., hosting, analytics, security), only to the extent necessary to provide the Service. We do not sell personal data.

8. Hosting

The Service is hosted by OVHcloud (France).

9. International transfers

Some providers may process data outside the European Economic Area. Where applicable, appropriate safeguards may be used (e.g., contractual protections).

10. Data retention

  • Contact messages: retained for up to 12 months after the last exchange, unless a longer period is required (e.g., to handle a dispute) or you request deletion earlier where applicable.
  • Analytics data: retained according to the analytics provider settings and your consent choices.

11. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, and portability. Where processing is based on consent, you can withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise your rights, please use the contact form. You may also lodge a complaint with your data protection authority.

12. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data. No system is 100% secure, and you use the Service at your own risk.

13. Third-party links

The Service may link to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices.

14. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date indicates the latest revision.

15. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please use the contact form.
