Countries in Southern Europe, particularly Spain, Greece, Portugal, Italy, and neighbors, emphasize the link between extreme heatwaves and intensified wildfire outbreaks. The governments are concerned about the potential role of arson but also stress climate change as a major factor worsening wildfire frequency and intensity. Overall, this viewpoint stresses multinational cooperation and enhanced emergency management to combat the growing wildfires.
