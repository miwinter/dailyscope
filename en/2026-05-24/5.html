White House shooting: suspect neutralized, investigation ongoing

US White House Shooting 2026-05-24 • 60 articles in total

In brief
  • A gunman fired near the White House and was killed by U.S. security forces after a rapid response.
  • At least one bystander was injured and hospitalized during the incident which led to a lockdown around the presidential residence.
  • The attacker, identified as a 21-year-old with mental-health issues, triggered panic while authorities investigate the motive and sequence of events.

A gunman opened fire near a White House security post and was engaged by U.S. security forces in a rapid response that left the suspect dead. Reports vary on details but several outlets say a bystander was injured and rushed to hospital, and the area around the presidential residence was placed on lockdown while investigators collected evidence. U.S. authorities, including the Secret Service and FBI, are leading an inquiry into the motive and sequence of events, and surveillance footage and witness video are being reviewed. Multiple international outlets identify the attacker as a 21-year-old, with several reports noting a history of mental-health issues; coverage also emphasizes scenes of panic and dozens of gunshots captured by reporters.
