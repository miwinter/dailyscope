Trump and Putin Plan Budapest Meeting to Address Ukraine Conflict

2025-10-17 • 88 articles in total

Former US President Donald Trump and Russian President Vladimir Putin have scheduled a high-profile meeting in Budapest focused on seeking an end to the ongoing war in Ukraine. This follows a series of productive phone calls between the two leaders, signaling renewed diplomatic efforts amidst global tensions. The planned summit is positioned alongside Trump's scheduled meeting with Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy, highlighting coordinated efforts for conflict resolution. Various international observers and political figures have expressed cautious optimism, while emphasizing the significant geopolitical implications of this engagement. The meeting is viewed as a crucial step in exploring potential peace talks, with agenda details yet to be disclosed.
