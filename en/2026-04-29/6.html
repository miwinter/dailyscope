Indictment of James Comey over alleged threat to Trump

US Politics: Comey Indictment 2026-04-29 • 19 articles in total

In brief
  • FBI director James Comey was criminally indicted for an alleged threat to President Trump using a symbolic Instagram post.
  • Coverage highlights legal procedures and political tensions tied to the indictment during the Trump era.
  • The case reflects broader issues of social media's legal interpretation and ongoing federal investigations.

Multiple international outlets report that FBI director James Comey has been criminally indicted for an alleged threat to the life of President Donald Trump, stemming from a social media Instagram post featuring seashells arranged as '86 47'. Coverage focuses on the charges, procedural steps including an arrest warrant in some reports, and possible penalties if convicted. Commentators and some reports frame the move within the broader context of legal actions and political tensions from the Trump era, with Trump himself criticizing the charges as politically motivated. Other pieces highlight related angles: how social media symbolism is interpreted legally, a separate DOJ indictment of a Fauci adviser, and a tangential regulatory story about U.S. media oversight amid political disputes.
