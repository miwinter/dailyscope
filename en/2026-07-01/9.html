Pakistan tuition-centre roof collapse kills 14 children

Pakistan Roof Collapse 2026-07-01 • 6 articles in total

In brief
  • A roof collapsed at a tutoring centre near Lahore, killing 14 children and injuring others.
  • Emergency teams rescued survivors and transported the injured to hospitals.
  • Authorities opened investigations and calls increased for safety inspections and better building standards in educational facilities.

A roof at a tutoring/learning centre near Lahore collapsed, killing 14 children and injuring others. Emergency responders and rescue teams rushed to the scene, pulled survivors from the rubble and transported the injured to hospitals. Authorities have opened investigations and officials face pressure to determine the cause and whether safety or maintenance lapses at the facility contributed. The tragedy has prompted wider calls for safety inspections, improved building standards and accountability for crowded or informal education sites across Pakistan.
