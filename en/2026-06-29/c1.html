The extreme temperatures have led to infrastructure damage, increased hospital admissions, and tragic incidents including drownings and a child’s death in a locked car. Authorities have issued health advisories, mobilized cooling measures in schools, and emphasized the need for climate adaptation and preparedness amid ongoing climate change challenges.
