Niamey airport attack: casualties, jihadist claims and regional response

West Africa Security Diplomacy 2026-06-19 • 17 articles in total

In brief
  • Militant groups linked to jihadist or Al-Qaeda factions carried out a deadly attack on Niamey's airport.
  • Local authorities maintained airport operations while securing the site and investigating the incident.
  • Neighboring states and international partners increased security cooperation in response to the attack.

Multiple outlets report a deadly assault on Niamey’s Diori Hamani International Airport with widely varying casualty figures in preliminary tallies. Several sources attribute the raid to jihadist or Al-Qaeda-linked groups and some outlets relay claims of responsibility. Local authorities emphasized that the airport remained operational while security forces secured the site, investigations continued, and residents were urged to stay alert amid reports of gunfire and explosions. Neighbouring states and international partners have voiced solidarity and signalled increased security cooperation in the Sahel as the situation remains fluid.
