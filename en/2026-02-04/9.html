Fatal Collision Between Migrant Boat and Greek Coast Guard in Aegean Sea

Greece Migrant Boat Collision 2026-02-04 • 11 articles in total

In brief
  • A collision between a migrant boat and Greek coast guard near Chios caused at least 14 deaths and ongoing rescue operations.
  • The exact number of migrants onboard is unknown and investigations into the collision are in progress.
  • The incident highlights the severe risks migrants face and the wider humanitarian crisis in the Mediterranean region.

A tragic collision occurred in the Aegean Sea near the island of Chios between a migrant boat and a Greek coast guard vessel, resulting in at least 14 to 15 deaths. The exact number of people onboard the migrant boat remains uncertain, and rescue operations are ongoing amid challenging conditions. Authorities from various countries emphasize the dangers faced by migrants attempting sea crossings to reach Europe, highlighting the broader humanitarian crisis in the Mediterranean region. Investigations into the cause of the collision are underway, with some reports noting the coast guard had requested the migrant boat to change course prior to the accident. This incident underscores the persistent risks and tensions surrounding migration management in the area.
