Devastating Earthquake in Afghanistan: Humanitarian Crisis and International Response

2025-09-01 • 109 articles in total

A powerful magnitude 6.0 earthquake struck eastern Afghanistan near Jalalabad, causing widespread devastation with death toll estimates ranging from hundreds to over 600 fatalities and more than 1,500 injuries. Rescue and relief operations are ongoing amid challenging mountainous terrain, damaged infrastructure, and ongoing aftershocks, complicating access to affected remote communities. The disaster exacerbates an already critical humanitarian situation in the region, prompting numerous calls from international aid organizations and governments for urgent assistance. Countries emphasize the need for coordinated aid efforts alongside local authorities, particularly the Taliban-led administration, to mitigate the disaster's impact and facilitate recovery.
