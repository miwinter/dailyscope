Deadly School Shooting in Canada Sparks National Mourning and Calls for Safety

Canada Mass Shooting 2026-02-13 • 18 articles in total

In brief
  • A mass shooting in British Columbia killed eight to ten people including an 18-year-old female suspect and caused national mourning in Canada.
  • Authorities are investigating the suspect's mental health, firearm licenses, and background while communities call for better safety and gun control.
  • The incident sparked debates on public security and mental health support, with leaders expressing condolences and pledging action.

A tragic mass shooting in a remote British Columbia town has resulted in the deaths of eight to ten people, including an 18-year-old female suspect. The incident, one of the deadliest in Canadian history, has deeply shocked the nation and prompted widespread mourning. Authorities are investigating the suspect's background, including mental health issues and prior firearm licenses, while the community grapples with grief and calls for improved safety measures. The event has reignited debates on gun control, mental health support, and public security in Canada. National leaders, including the Prime Minister, have expressed condolences and pledged to address the underlying issues highlighted by this tragedy.
