Intensified Russian Attacks Deepen Humanitarian Crisis in Ukraine Amid Stalled Peace Talks

Russia Shelling Ukraine 2026-02-13 • 17 articles in total

In brief
  • Russian forces have intensified attacks on Ukrainian civilian and military infrastructure, causing significant casualties and service disruptions.
  • Peace negotiations continue amid ongoing violence, complicating diplomatic efforts and increasing humanitarian concerns.
  • International reactions vary from condemning attacks and emphasizing humanitarian impact to focusing on military developments and diplomatic tensions.

Russian forces have escalated attacks on Ukrainian cities, targeting critical infrastructure such as energy and water supplies, leaving hundreds of thousands without basic services. These strikes have caused significant civilian casualties, including children and infants, highlighting the severe human toll of the conflict. Despite ongoing US-brokered peace negotiations, military aggression continues unabated, complicating diplomatic efforts. International bodies like the UN have condemned attacks on civilian infrastructure, urging restraint to prevent further humanitarian deterioration. The situation remains volatile with both sides preparing for renewed talks while violence persists on multiple fronts.
