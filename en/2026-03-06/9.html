A Bolivian military plane carrying a large amount of cash or banknotes crashed, resulting in fatalities ranging from 15 to 22 according to various reports. The crash has raised serious concerns about aviation safety, security protocols, and corruption within the military. Following the accident, there were incidents of looting at the crash site, prompting authorities to use tear gas to control crowds. Investigations are ongoing to determine the cause of the crash and to address the broader implications for military logistics and transport security in Bolivia. Emergency responses included rescue operations and hospital blood donation drives to treat the injured.

========

The United Nations has issued multiple urgent warnings about severe humanitarian crises unfolding across the Middle East and neighboring regions due to ongoing conflicts. Massive displacement affects hundreds of thousands, with refugee numbers soaring in Iran, Lebanon, Afghanistan-Pakistan border areas, and Gaza, straining regional resources. Attacks on civilians, including schools and health facilities, have resulted in significant casualties, notably among children, prompting calls for investigations and protection of humanitarian workers. The suspension of medical aid operations and looming funding shortages for food assistance further exacerbate the dire situation. UN officials emphasize the need for immediate ceasefires, coordinated humanitarian responses, and increased international support to prevent further deterioration.
