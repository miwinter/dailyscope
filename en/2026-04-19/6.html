Kyiv shooting sparks terror probe and Russia-origin claims

Kyiv Terror Attack 2026-04-19 • 27 articles in total

In brief
  • A gunman with Russian ties attacked Kyiv, killing around six and taking hostages before police shot him dead.
  • Authorities are investigating the attack as a possible act of terrorism amid Russia–Ukraine tensions.
  • Reports vary but include claims the attacker was a Russian military officer, raising political concerns.

A gunman opened fire in Kyiv, killing multiple people, taking hostages in a supermarket and other locations, and was ultimately shot dead by police. Most reports give a death toll of around six with numerous injuries, though early accounts varied. Ukrainian authorities have launched investigations and several outlets report the attack is being treated as a possible act of terrorism. Many sources highlight that the suspect was born in Moscow or had Russian ties, a detail that intensified concerns given the broader Russia–Ukraine tensions.
