2025-11-06 • 68 articles in total

Humanitarian concerns are underscored by visits from global figures and calls for increased international aid, as well as reports of harsh battlefield conditions. The conflict remains highly volatile, with escalating violence, economic warfare, and persistent international divisions shaping its trajectory.
