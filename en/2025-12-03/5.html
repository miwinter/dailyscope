Arrest of Federica Mogherini in EU Fraud Investigation

Mogherini Fraud Probe 2025-12-03 • 23 articles in total

In brief
  • Federica Mogherini and other EU officials were arrested in Belgium for a fraud investigation involving EU funds and diplomatic programs.
  • The scandal involves alleged corruption and misuse of public procurement within EU diplomatic institutions.
  • The case has caused political repercussions and concerns about EU governance and credibility.

Federica Mogherini, former EU High Representative for Foreign Affairs, has been arrested in Belgium amid a fraud investigation involving European Union funds and a diplomatic training program. The probe centers on alleged irregularities and corruption linked to tenders at the College of Europe and the EU diplomatic service. Multiple sources report raids and detentions of other high-ranking officials alongside Mogherini, highlighting a significant scandal within EU institutions. While details remain limited, the case has sparked political repercussions and concerns about governance and integrity in European diplomacy. The investigation is ongoing, with authorities and EU bodies closely monitoring developments.
