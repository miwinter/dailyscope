US Military Strikes on Venezuela Escalate Regional and Global Tensions

US–Venezuela Air and Military Strikes 2026-01-05 • 71 articles in total

In brief
  • The US launched planned military strikes on Venezuela using advanced drones to target Nicolás Maduro's regime causing many casualties.
  • The strikes caused civilian deaths and heightened regional instability, drawing condemnation from Venezuela, Cuba, and many international actors.
  • Global reactions vary with some supporting the US intervention while others call for diplomacy to prevent escalation and respect sovereignty.

The United States launched significant military strikes on Venezuela targeting President Nicolás Maduro's regime, resulting in numerous casualties including Venezuelan civilians and Cuban military personnel. The operation, reportedly planned over several months, involved advanced drone technology and large-scale air assaults, leading to widespread destruction and heightened instability. International reactions are deeply divided: while some countries and leaders condemn the US actions as violations of sovereignty and international law, others, including Israel, support the intervention as a stand against dictatorship. Regional actors like Colombia and CARICOM call for diplomatic dialogue to prevent further escalation, while global powers such as Russia and China criticize the US move, warning of broader geopolitical consequences. The crisis has prompted urgent UN Security Council meetings and widespread calls for de-escalation amid fears of a growing humanitarian and geopolitical crisis.
