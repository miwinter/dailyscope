Escalating Diplomatic Tensions Between US and Colombia Over Visa Revocation of President Gustavo Petro

2025-09-28 • 46 articles in total

The United States revoked the visa of Colombian President Gustavo Petro following his participation in a pro-Palestinian protest in New York and his calls for US soldiers to disobey former President Donald Trump. The US government cited Petro's remarks and actions as "reckless," "incendiary," and provocative, escalating diplomatic tensions between the two nations. In response, Petro and Colombia accused the US of violating international law and United Nations immunity, while urging diplomatic prudence to preserve bilateral ties. This visa revocation reflects broader geopolitical conflicts tied to the Israel-Palestine issue and deteriorating US-Colombia relations amid strong political rhetoric.
