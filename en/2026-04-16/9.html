Democrats move to impeach U.S. defense secretary

US Defense Impeachment 2026-04-16 • 6 articles in total

In brief

  • The impeachment effort is framed as a formal congressional process with partisan conflict, with Republicans dismissing it as politically motivated.
  • The case raises national security concerns, executive war powers, and oversight issues amid tensions with Iran.

International reports show U.S. Democrats filing or pursuing impeachment proceedings against Secretary of Defense Pete Hegseth over alleged misconduct tied to wartime actions.
