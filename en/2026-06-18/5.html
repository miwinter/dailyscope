Russo-Ukrainian War 2026-06-18 • 60 articles in total

In brief
  • Russian missile and drone strikes target Kyiv and Ukrainian cities while Ukrainian forces conduct cross-border attacks inside Russia, including on refineries and logistics nodes.
  • Western and G7 leaders deepen military aid to Ukraine and tighten sanctions on Russia, reflecting united diplomatic efforts against Moscow.
  • The conflict causes significant civilian casualties, cultural site damages, political assassinations abroad, and escalating maritime and drone warfare raising regional security concerns.

Across multiple reports, the war shows intensified kinetic activity: Russian missile and drone strikes continue to hit Kyiv and other Ukrainian cities while Ukrainian drones and strikes increasingly hit targets inside Russia, including refineries and logistics nodes. Western and G7 leaders are publicly deepening military assistance and tightening sanctions, reflecting a growing diplomatic consensus to bolster Kyiv and pressure Moscow. Independent analyses and open-source claims highlight strains on Russian weapon stocks and evolving drone warfare, while maritime incidents and warning shots by Russian warships raise regional security concerns. The fighting’s human toll — civilian deaths, damage to religious heritage sites, contested cross-border attacks on buses, and politically charged killings abroad — has provoked international condemnation and heightened tensions across allied capitals.
