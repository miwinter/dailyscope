Outcry and inquiry after student killed and handcuffed

UK Murder Investigation 2026-06-03 • 8 articles in total

In brief
  • An 18-year-old student named Henry Nowak was fatally stabbed while handcuffed, sparking national outrage and widespread video circulation.
  • Released footage and bodycam images intensified scrutiny on police actions, prompting official investigations and calls for transparency and accountability.
  • Large protests erupted in southern England, with clashes between demonstrators and police raising concerns over public order and extremist involvement.

Henry Nowak, an 18-year-old student, was fatally stabbed and footage showing him handcuffed while pleading for help has been widely circulated, prompting national outrage. Newly released video and bodycam images have intensified scrutiny of police actions and led to official promises of investigations and procedural reviews. Large protests erupted in southern England and some demonstrations descended into clashes with police, raising concerns about public order and extremist involvement. Political leaders, campaigners and community figures have demanded transparency and accountability while urging calm amid heated debate over race, policing and use of force.
