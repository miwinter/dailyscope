Military Expansion and Defense Strategy

France is significantly bolstering its military posture by increasing its nuclear warheads for the first time in decades, ceasing disclosure of stockpiles, and extending nuclear deterrence to European allies. The deployment of the aircraft carrier Charles de Gaulle to the Mediterranean and fighter jets to Middle Eastern bases underscores France's commitment to regional stability. The unveiling of the advanced nuclear submarine 'The Invincible' and the movement of military assets to Cyprus alongside Greece and the UK highlight France's strategic modernization and response to escalating global tensions.
