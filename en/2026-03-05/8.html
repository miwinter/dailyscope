Ecuador Expels Cuban Diplomatic Mission Amid Rising Tensions

Ecuador Expels Cuban Envoy 2026-03-05 • 15 articles in total

In brief
  • Ecuador expelled the Cuban diplomatic mission and withdrew its ambassador from Havana, escalating bilateral tensions.
  • Cuba condemned Ecuador's actions as arbitrary and disrespectful to diplomatic norms.
  • The diplomatic break reflects broader regional tensions and shifting political dynamics in Latin America.

Ecuador has expelled the Cuban ambassador and the entire Cuban diplomatic mission, giving them 48 hours to leave the country, marking a significant diplomatic escalation. The Ecuadorian government also withdrew its ambassador from Havana, signaling a severe deterioration in bilateral relations. While Ecuador has not fully disclosed the reasons, reports mention incidents such as embassy personnel burning documents and broader political disagreements. Cuba strongly condemned the expulsion as arbitrary and unjustified, emphasizing the importance of diplomatic respect. This diplomatic rupture reflects growing regional tensions and shifting political dynamics in Latin America.
