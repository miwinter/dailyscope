US Submarine Sinks Iranian Warship off Sri Lanka, Escalating Regional Tensions

IRIS Dena Sub Attack 2026-03-05 • 19 articles in total

In brief
  • A US submarine torpedoed and sank the Iranian frigate IRIS Dena near Sri Lanka, causing significant casualties and many missing sailors.
  • The incident is a rare, serious escalation in US-Iran military tensions extending conflict to the Indian Ocean and raising regional security concerns.
  • Sri Lankan authorities conducted rescue operations highlighting humanitarian impacts and prompting global calls for restraint amid heightened instability.

A US submarine torpedoed and sank the Iranian frigate IRIS Dena near Sri Lanka, resulting in significant casualties with reports of at least 80 to over 100 missing or dead sailors. The incident marks a rare and serious escalation in US-Iran military confrontations, extending conflict dynamics into the Indian Ocean region. Sri Lankan authorities have conducted rescue operations, recovering survivors and bodies, while the attack has raised widespread humanitarian and maritime security concerns. The event has drawn international attention, highlighting the risks of broader regional instability and prompting calls for restraint amid ongoing tensions.
