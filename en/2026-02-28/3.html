The Epstein scandal has led to extensive congressional hearings, with US Presidents Bill and Hillary Clinton testifying to deny any knowledge or involvement in Epstein's criminal activities. Bill Clinton consistently asserted he saw nothing wrong and was unaware of Epstein's offenses, while Hillary Clinton denied any connection or knowledge of Epstein. Political tensions surfaced, with Donald Trump expressing displeasure over Clinton's testimony and Clinton calling for Trump to be questioned. The scandal's repercussions extended globally, prompting resignations such as Borge Brende's from the World Economic Forum and investigations into officials in countries like Kazakhstan. The ongoing investigations continue to unravel Epstein's network and its impact on prominent figures and institutions worldwide.
