Bolivian Military Plane Crash Causes Significant Fatalities and Raises Safety Concerns

Bolivia Military Plane Crash 2026-02-28 • 25 articles in total

In brief
  • A Bolivian military plane carrying banknotes crashed near El Alto, causing at least 15 deaths and many injuries.
  • Bystanders tried to collect scattered cash, hindering rescue efforts and requiring police intervention.
  • Investigations consider weather and technical failure as causes and raise concerns about military transport safety.

A Bolivian military plane carrying freshly printed banknotes crashed near El Alto, close to La Paz, resulting in at least 15 fatalities and numerous injuries. The crash scattered cash across a busy road, prompting bystanders to attempt to collect the money, which complicated rescue efforts and required police intervention. The incident has drawn attention to aviation safety and security protocols within Bolivia's military transport operations. Investigations are ongoing to determine the cause, with adverse weather and technical failure among the considered factors. The tragedy has caused significant disruption locally and raised broader concerns about the risks of military cargo flights in the region.
