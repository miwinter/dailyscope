Trump's Push for UFO and Extraterrestrial Disclosure

Trump UFO Disclosure Actions 2026-02-20 • 21 articles in total

In brief
  • Trump ordered the release of UFO and extraterrestrial files to increase government transparency and respond to public interest.
  • His actions critique Obama's handling of classified information and mark a policy shift toward openness on unidentified aerial phenomena.
  • The disclosures sparked global media attention and ongoing debate but provided no confirmed evidence of alien contact.

U.S. President Donald Trump has taken significant steps to declassify and release government files related to UFOs and extraterrestrial life, aiming to increase transparency and address public curiosity. His directives often reference previous administrations, particularly criticizing President Obama for withholding or prematurely revealing classified information. The move has sparked widespread international media interest, highlighting a shift toward openness on unidentified aerial phenomena. While no definitive evidence of alien contact has been confirmed in the released files, the disclosures are expected to fuel ongoing debates and scientific inquiry. Trump's actions represent a notable policy change in the handling of UFO-related government information.
