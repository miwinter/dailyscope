Deadly Antisemitic Terror Attack at Bondi Beach, Australia

Australia Anti-Semitic Shooting 2025-12-15 • 65 articles in total

In brief
  • A terrorist attack during a Hanukkah event at Bondi Beach, Sydney, killed 16 and injured many, targeting the Jewish community.
  • The father-son attackers included one killed by police and one arrested; a Muslim bystander helped disarm a shooter.
  • The attack sparked global condemnation as antisemitic terror, raising concerns about rising hate crimes and calls for increased security.

A terrorist shooting attack targeted the Jewish community during a Hanukkah celebration at Bondi Beach in Sydney, Australia, resulting in 16 deaths and dozens injured. The perpetrators were identified as a father and son duo, with one killed by police and the other arrested. The attack has been widely condemned internationally as an antisemitic act, sparking global outrage and calls for increased security measures to protect Jewish communities. Heroic actions by bystanders, including a Muslim man who disarmed one of the shooters, were highlighted amid the tragedy. The incident has intensified discussions on rising antisemitism and terrorism in Australia and worldwide.
