Escalation of Iranian Attacks on Dubai and Abu Dhabi Airports Amid Regional Conflict

Dubai Regional Attacks and Explosions 2026-03-01 • 21 articles in total

In brief
  • Iranian missile and drone attacks struck key UAE infrastructure, causing casualties and suspending flights in Dubai and Abu Dhabi.
  • The UAE intercepted attacks and emphasized defensive actions amid growing regional tensions involving US and Israeli strikes on Iran.
  • Eyewitnesses reported explosions and panic, highlighting the conflict's impact on civilians and regional security instability.

A series of missile and drone attacks attributed to Iran have targeted key infrastructure in Dubai and Abu Dhabi, including international airports and luxury hotels, resulting in casualties and significant disruptions. These assaults have caused at least one death and multiple injuries, with flights suspended and airspace closed as security concerns mount. Eyewitness accounts describe explosions, fighter jets overhead, and widespread panic among civilians and tourists. The attacks are part of a broader regional escalation following US and Israeli strikes on Iran, intensifying tensions across the Gulf. The incidents highlight the fragile security environment and the expanding scope of conflict affecting both military and civilian targets in the United Arab Emirates.
