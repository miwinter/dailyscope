2025-09-30 • 81 articles in total

The series of articles collectively illustrate the announcement and promotion of a US-led 20-21 point peace plan for Gaza brokered primarily between former President Donald Trump and Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, who conditionally supports the initiative. The plan emphasizes ceasefire, hostage release, formation of transitional governance structures, and economic stabilization, including proposals for a ‘Board of Peace’ and oversight mechanisms. While Israel and several international actors, including some Arab and Muslim states, cautiously support the plan, Palestinian factions such as Hamas and Palestinian Islamic Jihad categorically reject it, viewing it as biased toward Israeli interests. The peace plan remains highly controversial with mixed reactions, underscoring ongoing challenges in achieving a durable resolution to the Gaza conflict.
