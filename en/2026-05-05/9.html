Stranded cruise off Cape Verde amid suspected hantavirus; reports diverge on severity

Cape Verde Health Risk 2026-05-05 • 8 articles in total

In brief
  • A cruise ship with about 150 people is stranded off Cape Verde amid suspected hantavirus exposure and ongoing investigations.
  • Reports vary from no confirmed cases on land to multiple illnesses and three deaths onboard, with strict containment and evacuation measures enforced.

A cruise ship carrying roughly 149–150 passengers and crew is stranded off Cape Verde amid concerns about a suspected hantavirus exposure. Some reports emphasize caution: authorities are monitoring the vessel, investigating possible exposures, and preparing evacuations while noting no confirmed land cases. Other outlets describe a more severe picture, citing multiple illnesses and three reported deaths aboard and strict restrictions on disembarkation and hospital access by Cabo Verde. The incident has drawn international attention, prompted quarantine and evacuation measures for some passengers, and left health and maritime authorities coordinating containment and further investigation.
