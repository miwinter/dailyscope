France Responds to Drone Incursions Over Nuclear Submarine Base

France Drone Incursion 2025-12-06 • 11 articles in total

In brief
  • France faced multiple drone incursions at its nuclear submarine base, prompting military firing and missile defense deployment.
  • The incidents raised national security concerns about espionage, surveillance, and hybrid warfare from possible foreign actors.
  • French authorities are actively investigating the origin and intent of the drones to strengthen defense of critical infrastructure.

France has experienced multiple drone incursions over its nuclear submarine base, prompting heightened security measures including military firing on the drones and the establishment of a missile defense base. The incidents have raised serious national security concerns about surveillance, espionage, and potential hybrid warfare threats. French authorities are actively investigating the origin and intent of these drones, with some speculation pointing towards foreign actors. These events highlight the vulnerability of critical military infrastructure to unmanned aerial threats and France's commitment to strengthening its defense capabilities.
