US-Iran Nuclear Diplomacy: Tensions, Negotiations, and Regional Implications

US–Iran Nuclear Diplomacy 2026-02-02 • 146 articles in total

In brief
  • US and allies pursue diplomacy with Iran while maintaining military pressure and addressing regional security concerns.
  • Iran engages in nuclear talks but excludes missile program discussions and warns against attacks to protect sovereignty.
  • Regional actors highlight risks of incomplete deals and the fragile Middle East conflict impacts US-Iran diplomacy.

The US-Iran nuclear diplomacy landscape remains highly tense yet cautiously hopeful, with President Donald Trump expressing optimism about reaching a deal despite warnings from Iran's Supreme Leader about potential regional war. Multiple reports confirm ongoing serious talks and diplomatic engagements, including meetings organized in Ankara and confirmed dialogues, though key issues like Iran's defense capabilities remain contentious. The US maintains a strong military presence in the region as leverage, while Iran signals readiness for negotiations but insists on red lines, particularly excluding missile program discussions. Regional actors such as Israel express concerns over incomplete deals and security implications, while international stakeholders watch for progress amid fluctuating conflict dynamics. The situation is further complicated by related geopolitical tensions involving Cuba, Venezuela, and broader Middle East conflicts, underscoring the fragile balance between diplomacy and military posturing.

Iran's Position: Willingness to Negotiate with Firm Red Lines

Iran's viewpoint emphasizes readiness to engage in serious nuclear talks with the US while firmly excluding negotiations on its ballistic missile program and defense capabilities. Iranian officials express a preference for diplomacy to avoid war but issue stern warnings about regional conflict if attacked. This stance reflects a strategic balance between openness to dialogue and maintaining sovereignty over critical security issues, signaling cautious engagement amid ongoing tensions.
