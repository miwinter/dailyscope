Escalation of Israeli Military Strikes on Hezbollah and Gaza

Israel Military Strikes on Hezbollah and Gaza 2026-02-02 • 15 articles in total

In brief
  • Israeli military operations have targeted Hezbollah leaders and conducted heavy airstrikes causing significant Palestinian civilian casualties in Gaza.
  • These attacks have increased regional tensions and threatened ceasefire agreements, complicating humanitarian efforts.
  • International reactions are divided between security concerns and humanitarian calls for de-escalation.

Recent Israeli military operations have intensified, targeting Hezbollah leadership in southern Lebanon and conducting heavy airstrikes in Gaza. These strikes have resulted in significant casualties, including dozens of Palestinians, many of whom are civilians, and the killing of senior Hezbollah figures. The attacks have heightened regional tensions, threatening the fragile ceasefire agreements and complicating humanitarian efforts. International reactions vary, with some emphasizing security concerns and others highlighting the humanitarian impact and calling for de-escalation. The situation remains volatile, with ongoing risks of further escalation and instability in the Middle East.
