Iran's Stern Warnings Against US Military Intervention

Iran Threatens US Intervention 2026-02-02 • 30 articles in total

In brief
  • Iran's Supreme Leader warns any US attack will cause a large regional war with catastrophic effects and vows strong retaliation.
  • The US has deployed significant military forces near Iran, highlighting strategic complexities and risks in the region.
  • US officials express mixed reactions while Iran condemns Western powers and designates EU forces as terrorists amid rising tensions.

Iran's Supreme Leader Ayatollah Ali Khamenei and other top officials have issued repeated and strong warnings that any US military attack on Iran would trigger a large-scale regional war with catastrophic consequences. These statements come amid a significant US military buildup in the Middle East, including the deployment of the USS Abraham Lincoln carrier strike group. Iranian authorities emphasize their readiness to respond immediately and with unprecedented measures to any aggression, while also highlighting the risks of widespread instability. The rhetoric reflects escalating tensions and a firm Iranian stance against US intervention, with some Iranian leaders also condemning Western powers and affirming their right to confront threats. US responses, including from President Trump, have ranged from dismissive to cautious, underscoring the fragile and volatile nature of the situation.
