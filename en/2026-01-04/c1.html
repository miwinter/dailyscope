France's Diplomatic and Political Involvement in Venezuela

French President Emmanuel Macron has publicly acknowledged the end of the Maduro dictatorship and advocated for a peaceful, democratic transition led by Venezuelan opposition figures. France has condemned the US military operation that captured Maduro, viewing it as a breach of international law, and has engaged in diplomatic talks with key international actors to address the crisis. This stance underscores France's emphasis on sovereignty, legal norms, and political solutions in Latin America amid heightened global tensions.
