Geopolitical Tensions Over Greenland and Arctic Sovereignty

French officials emphasize the importance of respecting Greenland's autonomy and sovereignty, advocating that its future should be decided by its people and Denmark. This diplomatic effort reflects broader concerns about Arctic strategic control and the need to counterbalance unilateral moves by the United States, highlighting France's commitment to multilateralism and regional stability in the Arctic.
