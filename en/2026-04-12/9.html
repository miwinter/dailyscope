Machete attack at New York transit hub

New York Knife Attack 2026-04-12 • 7 articles in total

In brief
  • A machete attack in New York's transit system injured three people before the suspect was fatally shot by police.
  • Authorities have released few details and the motive remains under investigation after the incident.
  • The attack disrupted service at a major transport hub and raised concerns about urban public safety.

Multiple outlets report a machete attack in New York's transit system—accounts reference Grand Central Station and the subway—where a suspect injured three people before being fatally shot by police. Some reports quote the assailant calling himself 'Lucifer', but authorities have released few details and the motive remains under investigation. The incident disrupted service at a major transport hub and renewed concerns about public safety on urban transit. Coverage varies in emphasis, with some sources highlighting police justification and ignored commands while others stress emergency response and the restoration of routine operations.
