US-EU Trade and Greenland Dispute

This cluster covers the escalating trade conflict between France (and other European countries) and the United States over Greenland. The US, under President Trump, threatened and imposed tariffs on several European nations including France to pressure support for a US acquisition of Greenland. France, led by President Macron, strongly opposes these coercive measures, calling for the activation of the EU's anti-coercion instrument and proposing retaliatory tariffs, signaling a historic rupture in transatlantic relations and a push for greater European strategic autonomy. The European countries have issued joint statements condemning the US actions and are coordinating diplomatic and economic responses to defend sovereignty and alliance cohesion.
