Deadly High-Speed Train Collision in Southern Spain

Spain Train Accident and Crash 2026-01-19 • 73 articles in total

In brief
  • A high-speed train derailment in southern Spain caused a collision killing between 21 and 39 people and injuring many more.
  • Authorities and international leaders express shock and are investigating the unusual accident on a straight modern track.
  • Eyewitnesses describe the scene as horrific, highlighting the disaster's severe emotional and physical impact.

A catastrophic collision between two high-speed trains occurred in southern Spain, near Córdoba and Andalusia, resulting in a death toll ranging from 21 to 39 and injuring dozens to over 150 people. The accident involved a derailment of one train that collided with another on an adjacent track, causing severe destruction and chaos. Emergency services responded promptly with ongoing rescue and recovery operations, while authorities launched investigations to determine the cause of this unusually severe accident. The tragedy has drawn national mourning and international condolences, highlighting concerns about railway safety in Spain. Eyewitnesses described the event as horrific, with scenes likened to an earthquake or horror movie, underscoring the disaster's traumatic impact.
