Trump Commits to Ending Sudan Conflict Following Saudi Crown Prince's Request

2025-11-20 • 20 articles in total

Former U.S. President Donald Trump has pledged to actively work towards ending the ongoing civil war and atrocities in Sudan, following a direct request from Saudi Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman. This commitment marks a notable shift in Trump's foreign policy focus, emphasizing increased U.S. diplomatic engagement in the region. Sudanese leaders and opposition groups have welcomed this intervention, expressing hope for peace and stability. The initiative also involves cooperation with regional actors such as Saudi Arabia, the UAE, and Egypt, reflecting a multilateral approach to resolving the crisis. Despite the announcement, specific strategies and timelines for the peace efforts remain unclear, while the humanitarian situation continues to deteriorate.
