US Proposes Controversial Peace Plan Requiring Ukraine to Cede Territory and Reduce Military

2025-11-20 • 39 articles in total

The United States has proposed a secretive and controversial peace plan aimed at ending the war between Russia and Ukraine. Central to the plan are demands for Ukraine to cede significant territories, including the Donbass region, and to substantially reduce its military capabilities. The proposal has sparked widespread concern and resistance within Ukraine, as it appears to align closely with Russian demands and threatens Ukrainian sovereignty. Diplomatic efforts involve secret negotiations between the US and Russia, with limited involvement or approval from Ukraine and its European allies. The plan's reception remains mixed internationally, highlighting the complex geopolitical tensions and the challenges of achieving a lasting resolution to the conflict.
