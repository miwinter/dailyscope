Escalation of Israeli Strikes in Lebanon and Gaza Amid Ceasefire Attempts

2025-11-20 • 15 articles in total

Israeli military operations have intensified in both southern Lebanon and the Gaza Strip, resulting in dozens of casualties including civilians and children. These strikes are often framed by Israel as responses to rocket attacks or efforts to prevent militant group activities, such as those by Hamas and Hezbollah. Despite ceasefire agreements, airstrikes have continued, leading to heightened tensions and warnings of dangerous escalations from groups like Hamas. The violence has drawn international concern due to the humanitarian impact and the risk of broader regional instability. Perspectives on the conflict vary, with some sources emphasizing Israel's security rationale and others highlighting the civilian toll and ceasefire violations.
