Western Security Concerns and Diplomatic Pragmatism

Western countries such as the US, UK, and EU exhibit a cautious stance, carefully navigating between upholding national security and engaging with geopolitical rivals like China and Russia. The collapse of espionage cases related to China in the UK, the EU's restrictions on Russian diplomats, and the US’s fluctuating diplomatic approach towards Venezuela illustrate this balance. Additionally, the US and its allies actively promote military cooperation with partners like Pakistan and South Korea to contain perceived threats. These actions demonstrate a pragmatic security-oriented diplomacy often coupled with efforts to maintain dialogue where possible.
