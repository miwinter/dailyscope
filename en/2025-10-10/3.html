Russia presents narratives framing the war as justified territorial defense, highlighting military gains, and accusing Western nations and Ukraine of exacerbating conflict dynamics. This perspective emphasizes claims of Ukrainian support to extremist groups as part of a wider geopolitical confrontation. It reflects Moscow’s communications strategy aimed at legitimizing its actions and challenging Western narratives.
