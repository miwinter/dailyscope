The Israel-Palestine conflict continues to intensify with significant military operations, leadership changes, and escalating violence in Gaza and the West Bank. Meanwhile, psychological impacts on Israeli society and grassroots peace initiatives reflect the deep and multifaceted consequences of the ongoing conflict.
