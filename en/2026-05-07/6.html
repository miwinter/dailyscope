Ted Turner, CNN Founder and Media Pioneer, Dies at 87

Ted Turner Obituary 2026-05-07 • 31 articles in total

In brief
  • Ted Turner founded CNN and pioneered 24-hour television news, reshaping global media.
  • He was a philanthropist and conservationist with a complex, outspoken public persona.
  • Political leaders and the public have begun reacting to his death and legacy.

Ted Turner, the American media entrepreneur who launched CNN and popularized 24-hour television news, has died at age 87. Reports from around the world underline his role in building a global media empire that reshaped how news is produced and consumed. Many obituaries also stress his philanthropic commitments and conservation work, while others recall a brash, outspoken personality that provoked both admiration and controversy. Political reactions and public tributes, including notable commentary from U.S. President Donald Trump, have begun to surface as the media and public reflect on his complex legacy.
