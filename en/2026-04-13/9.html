Deadly stampede at Haiti’s Citadelle amid conflicting casualty counts

Haiti Stampede Casualties 2026-04-13 • 8 articles in total

In brief
  • A deadly stampede at Haiti's Citadelle Laferrière killed 25 to 30 people and injured dozens.
  • Haitian authorities launched investigations and rescue operations while determining the cause and addressing safety concerns.
  • International reports highlight the human toll and call for improved crowd control at popular sites.

A deadly stampede at the Citadelle Laferrière (also referred to as Citadelle Henri) in northern Haiti killed between 25 and 30 people and left dozens injured, according to differing reports. Haitian authorities and the national police have opened investigations and mobilized rescue and security responses while officials seek to determine the cause. International coverage highlights the human toll and growing concerns about crowd control and safety at popular tourist and celebration sites. Discrepancies in casualty figures and limited immediate details persist as inquiries continue.
