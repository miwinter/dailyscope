Escalating Russian Attacks Devastate Ukrainian Infrastructure and Civilians

Russia Strikes Ukraine Military Campaign 2026-02-10 • 27 articles in total

In brief
  • Russian military intensifies attacks on Ukrainian infrastructure causing severe civilian casualties and logistical disruptions.
  • Ukraine requests increased Western air defense support to counter persistent Russian drone and missile strikes.
  • Despite diplomatic efforts, the conflict remains volatile with ongoing military actions and disputed territorial control.

Russian military operations in Ukraine have intensified, focusing on strategic infrastructure such as energy, transport, and drone manufacturing facilities, severely disrupting Ukrainian logistics and civilian life. Numerous airstrikes and drone attacks have caused significant civilian casualties, including women and children, highlighting the humanitarian toll of the conflict. Ukrainian leadership, including President Zelenskyy, has called for increased Western support, particularly in air defense, to counter the persistent Russian offensives. While Russia claims territorial gains, reports indicate challenges and setbacks in their air campaign, with some criticism directed at Ukrainian leadership amid worsening frontline conditions. The ongoing violence continues despite diplomatic efforts, underscoring the conflict's complexity and the urgent need for international engagement.
